Welcome to Pune , a city full of history and modern life. Pune is famous for its forts, temples, and beautiful hills. It is known as the Oxford of the East because of its education culture.It is aperfect place to travel , learning and relaxation.

Pune is one of the most important and beautiful cities ofmaharashtra.It is known as the"Cultural Capitalof Maharashtra" and also as the "Oxford of the East"because ofits many famous educational institutions. Pune is situated on the banks of the Mula andMutha rivers andhas a pleasant climate compared to many other big cities.

Pune has arich and glorious history.It was the home of the great Maratharuler Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who laid the foundation of the Maratha Empire here.Many historic places like Shaniwar Wada and Sinhagad Fort remind us of its brave past and attract many tourists.Today,Pune is also a modern and fast growing city.It is a important center for information technology,education,and automobile industries.Many students from different parts of India and even from other countries come to Pune to study.This gives the city a young and energetic atmosphere.
